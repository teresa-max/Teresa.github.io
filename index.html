Description

Shrimp Scampi with Pasta is a classic Italian-American dish, combining tender pasta with juicy shrimp tossed in a butter-garlic and olive oil sauce. A splash of white wine and fresh lemon juice brightens the flavor, while red pepper flakes add just the right touch of spice. Finished with parsley and lemon wedges, this quick yet elegant recipe is perfect for weeknight dinners but impressive enough for guests. Want to learn how to make it? Follow along with the steps!

Ingredients

  • One 16-ounce package of linguine pasta
  • Two tablespoons of butter
  • Two tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
  • Two finely diced shallots
  • Two cloves of garlic, minced
  • A pinch of red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • One pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • A small pinch of kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Half a cup of dry white wine
  • Juice of one lemon
  • Another two tablespoons of butter
  • Another two tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
  • A quarter cup of fresh parsley leaves, finely chopped
  • About one teaspoon of extra-virgin olive oil, or more if you like

Directions

Step 1

Gather ingredients.

Step 2

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Cook the pasta until al dente, about 6 to 8 minutes, then drain and set aside

Step 3

In a large skillet, melt butter together with olive oil over medium heat.

Step 4

Add garlic and cook until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.

Step 5

Stir in the shrimp, white wine, and lemon juice, seasoning with salt and black pepper.
Cook until the shrimp are pink and opaque, about 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 6

Remove the shrimp with a slotted spoon and set aside on a plate.
Pour the white wine and lemon juice into the skillet, scraping up the browned bits.

Step 7

Add more butter to the skillet and whisk it into the wine‚Äìlemon mixture until the sauce emulsifies and becomes glossy.

Step 8

Return the pasta and shrimp to the skillet. Toss everything together with chopped parsley.
If desired, an extra pinch of red pepper flakes, making sure the sauce coats the pasta evenly.

Step 9

Plate the pasta, garnish with parsley and fresh lemon wedges, and dig in right away.
